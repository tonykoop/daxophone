# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, tongue profiles, fret maps, pickup placement coordinates, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic 3/4 product photograph of a finished daxophone prototype: a shaped hardwood tongue clamped in a clean block on a compact base, the free tongue edge prepared for bowing, a separate hand-held dax block with a smooth curved contact face resting beside it, a small reversible contact pickup and cable strain relief visible without dominating the object, satin oil finish on figured wood, dark metal clamp hardware, realistic tabletop scale, clean studio background, soft directional studio lighting, ~50mm lens, high detail, no labels.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic performance scene in a small studio workshop, a player bowing the edge of the wooden tongue while the other hand uses the dax block to shape vocal gliding tones, a compact amplifier or recording interface nearby, natural window light, spare wood tongue coupons and clamp test pieces on the bench, expressive but careful posture, realistic hands, warm wood grain, shallow depth of field, concept visualization only.

## Detail Prompt

Photorealistic macro close-up of the signature daxophone mechanism: the clamped end of a shaped wooden tongue held by a dark pressure block, fine grain running along the tongue, a bow hair just touching the prepared edge, the curved face of the dax block pressing lightly farther along the tongue, a small contact pickup mounted reversibly near the clamp, soft raking light, high detail, no measurement marks.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, guitar strings, frets, dimension callouts, fixed fretboard, unsafe splinters, impossible clamp geometry, extra fingers, or pickup wires passing through solid wood unrealistically.
